About Our Services

Cliffe Dekker Hofmeyr (CDH) is a premier South African corporate law firm providing comprehensive legal services to local and multinational clients across Africa, supported by a team of over 340 lawyers. We specialize in banking, finance, competition, employment, dispute resolution, environmental, and real estate law, with extensive expertise in mining, technology, and regulatory sectors.

Key Features

  • African Footprint: Strong presence in South Africa and across the continent, with offices in Johannesburg and Cape Town, as well as a presence in Kenya.
  • Government & Private Sector: Acts for governments, state-owned entities, and top-tier corporations.
  • Multidisciplinary Approach: Collaborates with auditing and consulting professionals to provide integrated solutions.
