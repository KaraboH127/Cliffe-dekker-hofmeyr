Competition Law & Antitrust

Our Competition Law practice has a proven track record of providing strategic advice and practical solutions to competition law issues for clients with business interests in South Africa, Kenya, Namibia, and the rest of Africa.

Services

Competition Law Services


Our team offers a comprehensive service that encompasses every aspect of competition law, including:
  • All aspects of the Competition Act
  • Ensuring compliance through tailored audits, best practice and training
  • Preparing merger notification documents
  • Guiding clients through opposed merger investigation proceedings and hearings
  • Advising in cartel enforcement proceedings, abuse of dominance matters and vertical arrangements
  • Initiating, defending and advising on complaint proceedings before the competition authorities
  • Advising on damages actions relating to anti-competitive conduct
  • Market inquiries.
  • Leniency applications and settlements
  • Appeals
