Environmental Law & Regulatory Compliance

Our practice has extensive experience in all aspects of environmental legislation and we advise clients from a myriad of industries and across both the private and public sector.

Services

Our practice has extensive experience in relation to permitting, compliance, enforcement, due diligence investigation, deal advice, administrative appeals and review proceedings in relation to:
  • Mining and mineral reprocessing.
  • Energy project developments, including nuclear, renewable, thermal, coal and LPG.
  • Climate change and carbon regulation.
  • Biodiversity and conservation, including bioprospecting, biodiversity offsets and protected areas.
  • Pollution and contamination.
  • Waste management, including recycling and treatment.
  • Pharmaceuticals, medicines and medical devices.
  • Health and safety.
  • Manufacturing, including cement and paper and pulp.
  • The petrochemical industry across the operational value chain.
  • Foodstuffs, cosmetics and disinfectants.
