Eskal Burger is the Chief Financial Officer of Cliffe Dekker Hofmeyr. About Eskal

Eskal joined Hofmeyr Herbstein and Gihwala (now Cliffe Dekker Hofmeyr) in 1994 as the Financial Manager. He was promoted to Chief Operating Officer in 1999 and appointed as Chief Financial Officer in September 2008.
